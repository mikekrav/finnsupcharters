Experience Cape Fear

Welcome to Finns Up Charters, your premier destination for unforgettable maritime adventures along the stunning North Carolina coast. Based in the historic port city of Wilmington, we offer authentic charter experiences that showcase the natural beauty and rich waters of the Cape Fear River and Atlantic Ocean.

Our experienced captains and crew are passionate about sharing the magic of coastal Carolina waters with visitors and locals alike. Whether you're seeking a peaceful sunset cruise, an exciting fishing expedition, or a customized tour of our pristine waterways, we provide safe, comfortable, and memorable experiences for all ages.

From dolphin watching and scenic harbor tours to deep-sea fishing adventures and private celebrations, Finns Up Charters combines local expertise with genuine hospitality to create the perfect day on the water. Let us show you why Wilmington's waters are truly something special.

Tours

Discover the beauty of Wilmington's waterways with our guided tours. From scenic river cruises to wildlife watching adventures, explore historic sites and natural wonders while learning about local maritime heritage from our experienced captains.

Location

Conveniently located in historic downtown Wilmington, our marina provides easy access to the Cape Fear River and Atlantic Ocean. Find us at the heart of the city's vibrant waterfront district, surrounded by restaurants, shops, and attractions.

About Us

With over 20 years of experience navigating Carolina waters, our Coast Guard licensed captains are passionate about sharing the magic of our coastal region. We pride ourselves on safety, local expertise, and creating unforgettable memories on the water.
