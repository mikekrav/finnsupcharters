Our Tours

At Finns Up Charters, we believe every adventure should be tailored to your preferences. Our tours are designed on a consultation basis, allowing us to customize each experience to meet your specific interests and needs. Whether you're seeking a peaceful morning on the water or an action-packed day of exploration, our tours typically range from 3 to 6 hours, giving you plenty of time to fully immerse yourself in the beauty of North Carolina's coastal waters.

Beach Tours

Explore pristine beaches and secluded coves along the Carolina coast. Our beach tours offer opportunities for shelling, swimming, and discovering hidden gems accessible only by boat. Perfect for families and nature lovers seeking a peaceful escape on pristine sandy shores.

Fishing Trips

Experience world-class fishing in the rich waters off Wilmington. From inshore redfish and flounder to offshore marlin and mahi-mahi, our experienced captains know the best spots for every season. All skill levels welcome - we provide equipment and expertise.

River Cruises

Discover the historic Cape Fear River and its tributaries on our scenic river cruises. See historic battleships, charming waterfront communities, and abundant wildlife including dolphins, sea turtles, and coastal birds in their natural habitat.
